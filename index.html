This is Doctor App

Doctor App is you need if you’re not sure if you want to go to your GP but you’re not quite sure if you need to go. With Doctor App you can talk to a real doctor from anywhere you want. This saves you time without compromising on your personal health.

Why Doctor App?

Studies have shown that around 33% of visits to a doctor are not necessary. Of course many people still like to visit a doctor just to be sure. Doctor App helps contacting you through to an actual doctor while saving you the time you would normally spend waiting in a waiting room.

Meet your GP when it suits you

Doctor App can be used anywhere whenever it suits you! Whether you're on the bus home or just home. Just open your Doctor App and contact a doctor.
